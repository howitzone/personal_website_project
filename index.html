who am i?

Hello, I'm Howard Ticzon, an aspiring Freelance Web Developer

I am a Filipino-Canadian living in Surrey, BC, Canada.  As a course requirement, I created this website to showcase my skills using only HTML5 and CSS3, without any JavaScript.  My interest in learning web development stems from the high demand for these skills in the job market, which not only offers excellent earning potential but also provides job flexibility.  With a strong emphasis on work-life balance, I am motivated to pursue a career as a freelance web developer.

My journey to becoming a web developer is inspired by my background in electronics and communications engineering, as well as my strong passion for digital communications technology.  I am eager to enhance my skills through a comprehensive web design and development course.  This training will empower me to confidently create dynamic and impactful websites, enabling me to work as a freelance web developer.  I am committed to providing essential services to individuals and businesses that wish to establish a captivating online presence and succeed in the digital landscape.  Additionally, I aspire to start my own web design and development agency in the future.

Aside from working full-time during weekdays, I also pursue my passion for cycling, which keeps me active and energized.  Furthermore, I am a licensed amateur "ham radio" enthusiast with the callsign VA7HTZ.  I proudly incorporate my passion for communication into my work by using the suffix of my callsign as part of this website's name.
